How it works

Three simple steps

Choose your NYC spot

Any corner of the city that feels like yours — a rooftop, a library, the Brooklyn Bridge at dawn, a quiet neighbourhood you love.

Prepare an outfit that fits your chosen location and the way you want to remember this moment. No formal dress code — just you, intentionally.
