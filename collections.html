Eye Color Collection

Every pair of eyes holds a landscape. The Eye Color Collection begins with the most intimate detail about you — the color of the light you see the world through — and builds a flower around it.

Design Yours
Hazel Eyes
Warm amber, moss, honey gold

For the eyes that shift with light — sometimes gold, sometimes green, always warm. Silk petals in amber and burnished honey, with a touch of forest moss.

Blue Eyes
Sky blue, cornflower, pale ice

The blue that made our first customer stop and say — "those are my eyes." Soft blues, cool lilac, the particular clarity of a clear spring day.

Green Eyes
Sage, jade, spring green

The rarest eye color, and the hardest to capture. Sage silk, touches of jade, pale green petals that glow like leaves in early morning light.

Brown Eyes
Warm earth, coffee, bronze

The warmth of dark amber, the depth of earth after rain. Rich silk tones — bronze, deep rose gold, warm cocoa — for eyes that hold the whole world.

Grey Eyes
Silver mist, pewter, cloud

Cool, still, and luminous. Silks in soft grey, pale lavender, and dove white — for eyes with the quality of winter light on water.

"What color are your eyes? Tell us — and we will make a flower that has never existed before."
