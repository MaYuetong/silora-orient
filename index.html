A Story Worth Telling

The Spanish Pianist
and Her Eyes

She came to order orchid earrings in the colors of Central Park's cherry blossoms — the soft pinks of a New York spring. But when we looked up, we saw her eyes: deep, quiet, Mediterranean. She told us her story. We listened. And then we made something we had never made before — a piece not for the blossoms she had asked for, but for the sea she had left behind. That is how the 兰花系列 — the Orchid Collection — began.

Signature Craft

The Iris Brooch —
a flower held in time

Each iris brooch is hand-sculpted from dyed silk, wound with gold wire, and finished with a freshwater pearl. The petals catch the light the way real flowers do after rain. It takes hours to make a single piece — and that is exactly the point.

Traditional Chinese 缠花 craftsmanship, reimagined for a life lived across cultures.
