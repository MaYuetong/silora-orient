Intangible Cultural Heritage

非物质文化遗产

缠花 (Chanhua) is a silk wire-wrapping art originating in Hubei Province — officially recognized by China as an intangible cultural heritage, passed down through generations of artisans.

五

Five Years of Craft

五年匠艺

Our studio has spent five years mastering and evolving the traditional 缠花 technique — honoring its roots while shaping it for the lives and stories of women today.

女

Women-Led Studio

女性主创

A Story Worth Telling

The Spanish Pianist
and Her Eyes

She came to order orchid earrings in the colors of Central Park's cherry blossoms — the soft pinks of a New York spring. But when we looked up, we saw her eyes: deep, quiet, Mediterranean. She told us her story. We listened. And then we made something we had never made before — a piece not for the blossoms she had asked for, but for the sea she had left behind. That is how the 兰花系列 — the Orchid Collection — began.
