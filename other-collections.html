鸢尾胸针系列 · Brooch

Iris Brooch Series

A sculpture you can wear

Each iris brooch is hand-sculpted from dyed silk wound around gold wire — petals that curve the way real irises do, reaching upward with a quality that photographs cannot fully capture. The stem is a single curved gold wire finished with a freshwater baroque pearl.

Available in purple, white, powder blue, and blush. Custom colours on request. Each piece takes several hours to complete by hand.

Pearl Edition

Pure white silk orchid petals with gold-tone wire and a freshwater baroque pearl drop. The quietest piece in the collection — and often the one people wear most. It belongs everywhere.

Plum in Winter

The flower that blooms before spring arrives.

The plum blossom is the flower of endurance — it blooms in snow, before anything else dares to. This collection is for people who carry their softness through hard seasons. White and blush silk petals, wire stems, and the quiet insistence of something beautiful that refuses to wait.
